Ted's Famous Pad Thai

Description

With the right ingredients and preparation, you'll be able to make an authentic Thai dish that tastes like it came straight from Bangkok.

Here's how you can make authentic pad Thai and impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

    • Steps

    1. Cook noodles in boiling water until tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
    2. While noodles are cooking, heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large wok over high heat. Add choice of protein and cook, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer to a medium bowl.
    3. In same wok, heat 1 tablespoon oil.
    4. In a small bowl, whisk eggs until blended. Cook, stirring occasionally and breaking up curds with a spoon. Transfer to bowl with choice of protein.
    5. In a small bowl, whisk palm sugar, fish sauce, tamarind concentrate, lime juice, cayenne, 2 tablespoons oil, and 1 tablespoon water until combined.
    6. In same wok over medium-high heat, heat 2 tablespoons oil. Cook shallot and garlic, stirring frequently, until lightly golden, about 1 minute. Add scallions and cook, stirring frequently, until softened, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in sauce and bring to a simmer.
    7. Serve pad thai and enjoy!

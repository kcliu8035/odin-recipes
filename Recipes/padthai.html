How to Make Ted's Famous Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a delicious treat that combines just the right amount of flavor and sweetness.

Any type of pad thai is delicious, but getting that authentic Thai taste takes the right type of preparation and ingredients.

Here's how you can make your authentic pad Thai and impress your friends and family, leaving them wanting more and more.

This easy Pad Thai recipe has rice noodles, chicken, shrimp, tofu, peanuts, scrambled eggs, and fresh vegetables all tossed together in a delicious homemade sauce. It's packed with flavor and can be made in under 30 minutes!
